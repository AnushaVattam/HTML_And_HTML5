One of the most rewarding aspects of being a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas state University is the ability to work as a Graduate Instructional Assistant. This assistant ship was my first opportunity to run a classroom of my own. During my undergraduate career I participated as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant and enjoyed the opportunity to help students in the classes I had taken in Electronics and Communications.

Currently, I teach a variety of labs at different levels including C, C++,Digital Logic's depending on the semester.I find it rewarding to challenge myself to teaching something I do not have a background in before. I not only get to learn new topics but impart this newly acquired knowledge to my students!

My experience in programming at Texas State and during my previous experience as an “Process Analyst” at Deloitte has enabled me to develop technical, professional skills like C, C++, JAVA, Python, PHP, Perl Databases, CSS, JavaScript, HTML, ETL tools, Weka, Team work. Now as a Teaching Assistant, where I teach and assist student in skills C, C++.
